Make an archive from Xcode, export the build products. Put the "Products" directory next to this file.

Set the version number below and run:

pkgbuild --root Products/ --identifier au.com.mtlabs.dump1090-mac --version 1 dump1090-mac.pkg --sign "3rd Party Mac Developer Installer: MAX TECHNOLOGY LABS PTY LTD (5VXZZ2GBMN)"
productbuild --distribution distribution.plist --resources . --package-path dump1090-mac.pkg dump1090-mac-installer.pkg --sign "3rd Party Mac Developer Installer: MAX TECHNOLOGY LABS PTY LTD (5VXZZ2GBMN)"
